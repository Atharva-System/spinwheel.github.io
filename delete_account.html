Delete Your SpinWheel Account

If you wish to delete your SpinWheel account and associated data, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Open the App

Launch the SpinWheel application on your mobile device.

Step 2: Log In

Sign in to your account using your preferred OAuth provider (Google, Apple, or Yahoo).

Step 3: Access Settings

Navigate to the Settings page within the app. This is typically found in the menu or profile section.

Step 4: Go to Your Profile

Tap on "My Profile" within the Settings menu.

Step 5: Delete Account

Scroll to find the "Delete Account" option. Follow the prompts to confirm account deletion.

Important Note:

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data from our systems. This action cannot be undone. You will lose all your preferences, history, and any other information associated with your account.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues while trying to delete your account, please contact our support team at contact@atharvasystem.com.
